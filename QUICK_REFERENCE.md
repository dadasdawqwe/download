## FAQ

**Q: How many downloads at once?**
A: As many as you want! Backend handles unlimited.

**Q: Where do files save?**
A: Click [ğŸ“¥ Download] to save to your computer. Also saved on server.

**Q: Can I pause downloads?**
A: Not yet - they run to completion. Future feature planned.

**Q: Does queue save if I close browser?**
A: No - queue disappears on page refresh. Future feature planned.

**Q: Can I download from any website?**
A: Most sites work! YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, etc.

**Q: Why different file sizes?**
A: Video quality (1080p = larger), audio bitrate (320kbps = larger)

**Q: How long does downloading take?**
A: Depends on file size and internet speed. Usually 2-30 minutes.

**Q: What about copyrighted content?**
A: App downloads anything accessible. Respect copyright laws of your country.

**Q: Can I move downloads to USB?**
A: Yes! Download to computer, then move files around normally.

**Q: Works on mobile?**
A: Yes! Responsive design. Touch-friendly buttons. Full functionality.
